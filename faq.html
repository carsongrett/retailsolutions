Frequently Asked Questions

What is Retail Solutions?

Retail Solutions is a free tool that connects retail tenants — such as small business owners and franchisees — with commercial real estate brokers who specialize in helping tenants find and secure retail space.

Who is this service for?

This service is for business owners looking to lease or renew retail space in the United States. Whether you're opening your first location or relocating an existing one, Retail Solutions can match you with an experienced broker in your market.

Is there any cost to use Retail Solutions as a tenant?

No — submitting your information and getting matched with a broker is completely free for tenants.

How does the matching process work?

Once you submit the tenant intake form, our system will analyze your information and match you with brokers who specialize in your industry and location. You'll then be connected with one or more brokers who can assist.

What happens after I submit my information?

Your information will be reviewed, and we'll connect you with qualified brokers who match your needs. You may receive an email or phone call directly from a broker, or we may reach out to clarify any details.

What kind of brokers do you work with?

We work with independent brokers and small agencies who have direct experience with retail leasing. All brokers must apply to join the platform and are reviewed before being listed.

Is my information secure?

Yes. We do not sell your data. Your information is only used to connect you with relevant professionals. You can view our full privacy policy linked at the bottom of the site.

I'm a broker — how can I join the platform?

Click the "Brokers – Join Here" button in the top-right corner of the site. You'll fill out an application with your details, including areas of expertise and regions served. Once reviewed and approved, you'll be added to our network.

Do you guarantee results?

While we strive to match tenants with qualified brokers, we can't guarantee specific outcomes. We do our best to ensure every connection is relevant and helpful.

Can I update my submission after sending it?

Not yet — but this feature is coming soon. For now, if you need to make a change, please resubmit the form or contact us directly.
